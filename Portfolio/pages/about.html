Hello world

I’m a third year Human Computer Interaction major with a minor in Computer Science at the University of California, San Diego. I’m also an aspiring UX Designer. I started my undergrad in Computer Science, but eventually found my way to HCI.


I believe that inclusive design and empathy can change the world and I'm on a mission to figure out how we can empathize with people we've never met. I also care deeply about public good and hope I can use design to do more.


I'm a passionate mental health advocate and when I’m not studying, I'm either reading away on Medium, pinning on Pinterest, or playing around with data visualizations.

"Design creates stories, and stories create memorable experiences, and great experiences have this innate ability to change the way in which we view our world. "
